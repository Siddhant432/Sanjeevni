Telehealth, Not Just a Quick-Fix for Chronic Care Management

Patients with chronic conditions are used to seeing their providers frequently and in person for treatment monitoring and compliance, but due to the isolation precautions necessitated by the pandemic, especially for those with chronic health conditions, many patients with chronic conditions have transitioned to online visits with their providers. Patients with conditions ranging from chronic pain, anxiety, and depression, to diabetes and high blood pressure are now being provided with more options for improved management of their conditions through digital health and remote monitoring technology.

The Impact of Lifestyle Choices on Healthy Aging

ViTel Health’s advanced tools, including Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) and Chronic Care Management (CCM), are designed to help you manage your health effectively, ensuring that your later years are as full of vitality as your earlier ones. While aging is inevitable, how we age is largely within our control. By making conscious lifestyle choices inspired by the world’s healthiest populations and leveraging the advanced care options provided by ViTel Health, you can close the gap between your lifespan and health span. Embrace the power of proactive health management today, and ensure that your later years are not just longer but filled with energy, vitality, and joy.

Enhancing Physical and Cognitive Wellbeing for a Better Health Span

Our bodies and minds are made for doing! Our exercising body releases many chemicals that will enhance our brains and prevent cognitive decline. Engaging in stress-reducing activities like Tai Chi, fun activities like dancing or Pickleball, and low-impact, supervised, strength exercises not only strengthens your body but also stimulates your mind, improving memory and cognitive sharpness. Similarly, mental exercises—such as puzzles or learning new skills—help keep your brain healthy and reduce the risk of cognitive decline. This holistic approach ensures that both body and mind remain in peak condition, allowing you to make the most of each day.
