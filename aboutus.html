About Sanjeevni


We blend our collective strengths into integrated, scalable solutions, providing an array of physician-driven digital health services



Who We Are?

At ViTel Health, our driving philosophy is —Happy Physicians, Healthy Patients. When faced with the detrimental effects of a fragmented and failing healthcare system, our founders, Doug Sumaraga and Dr. Brittany Busse, decided that the best way to improve the health of patients and communities would be to empower physicians to reclaim control over the care of their patients. At ViTel Health, we take our knowledge of high-tech solutions in digital health and combine that with a high-touch compassionate approach that fosters community among physicians and curiosity about discovering solutions that foster inclusivity and equity. With ViTel Health, physicians are independent but not alone.



Mission

Happy Physicians, Healthy Patients

Our services are designed for physicians to optimize their time, own their credentials, connect with colleagues, and be life-long learners through a digital-first platform. Whether that's creating a virtual independent practice, finding a new role or gig work, or building your network with other physicians and professionals, ViTel Health has you covered.

When physicians can focus on their expertise while effectively managing stress, they can be there for their patients and provide better care.



Vision

Drive the Future of Healthcare

  • Practice applied compassion by empowering others to act in their own best interest. We promote agency for physicians and patients, as well as within our organization.
  • Improve the health of communities by creating inclusive and equitable access to care for all health conditions (physical and mental). We aim to offer care that is transparently and fairly priced, accessible to all no matter geography, and that can be truly portable so that patients never have to worry about receiving fragmented care.
  • Be of service to physicians and the communities that they serve. We want to do the good that is right in front of us, no matter how big or small.
  • Stay curious in our pursuit of innovation. Artificial Intelligence, remote monitoring devices, and electronic medical records are only as valuable as they are useful to the physicians and patients they serve. We are dedicated to thoughtful inquiry and open-mindedness in our pursuit of the best in preventative and interventional care technology.
  • Be a credible source of information for both patients and physicians. We operate with integrity, wisdom, and transparency in healthcare and in business.
  • Stay centered and true to our authentic goals and core principles. In an ever-changing health and business landscape, we will constantly be evaluating new opportunities. Our core mission of “Happy Physicians, Healthy Patients” will always remain our True North, allowing us to be discerning in our choices.
